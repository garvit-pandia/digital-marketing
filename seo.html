Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

The art and science of improving your website's visibility in organic search results. SEO is not just about search engines; it's about making your site better for people.

On-Page SEO

Optimizing individual web pages to rank higher.

  • Keyword Research: Finding terms users search for (Search Volume vs Difficulty).
  • Content Quality: Creating valuable, relevant info (E-E-A-T: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).
  • Meta Tags: Optimizing Title and Descriptions for CTR.
  • Internal Linking: Connecting related content to pass link equity.

Off-Page SEO

Actions taken outside of your own website.

  • Backlinks: Earning links from high Domain Authority (DA) sites.
  • Social Signals: Likes and shares on social media (indirect factor).
  • Guest Blogging: Writing for other industry sites to build relationships.
  • Local Citations: N.A.P. (Name, Address, Phone) consistency.

Technical SEO

Backend optimizations that help spiders crawl and index your site.

  • Site Speed: Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS).
  • Mobile-Friendliness: Responsive design is a must.
  • Sitemaps & Robots.txt: Guiding the bots.
  • Schema Markup: Structured data for Rich Snippets.

The Future of SEO

Voice Search

With Siri and Alexa, queries are becoming longer and more conversational. Optimize for "Natural Language".

AI Overviews (SGE)

Search Generative Experience is changing how results appear. Being the "source of truth" is more important than ever.

Popular SEO Tools

Google Search Console: Monitor site health.
Ahrefs/SEMrush: Competitor analysis & keywords.
Screaming Frog: Technical site crawler.
Yoast/RankMath: WordPress plugins.
