Content Marketing

A strategic marketing approach focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience â€” and, ultimately, to drive profitable customer action.

The Content Lifecycle

1. Strategy & Planning

Before you write, you must plan.

  • Audience: Who are they? What are their pain points?
  • Editorial Calendar: Consistency is key. Plan topics months in advance.
  • Keyword Mapping: Assign SEO keywords to specific pages.

2. Content Creation

Producing high-quality assets.

  • The "Skyscraper" Technique: Find top-ranking content and create something 10x better.
  • Formats: Blogs, Videos, Podcasts, Infographics, Whitepapers.
  • Voice: Maintain a consistent brand personality.

3. Distribution & Distribution

Don't just publish and pray.

  • Owned Channels: Newsletter, Website.
  • Earned Media: PR, Guest Posts, Shares.
  • Paid Media: Social Ads, Native Advertising.

Content Repurposing

Squeeze every drop of value from your work.

Turn a Blog Post into:

  • A Twitter thread
  • A LinkedIn carousel
  • A YouTube script
  • An Infographic
  • An Email newsletter intro

Storytelling Frameworks

The Hero's Journey: Your customer is the hero, your brand is the guide.

Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS): Identify a problem, outline why it's bad, offer the fix.

Before-After-Bridge: Show the current state, the desired future, and how your product bridges the gap.

Measuring Success

Consumption: Pageviews, Video views.

Retention: Time on page, Bounce rate.

Sharing: Social shares, backlinks.

Leads: Newsletter signups, Gated content downloads.

Why Content Wins

Content marketing costs 62% less than traditional marketing and generates about 3 times as many leads. By providing value upfront, you earn the right to sell later.

Distribution Strategy

Creating content is only half the battle. You must have a plan to distribute it.

Owned Media

Your website, blog, and email list. You have full control.

Earned Media

Shares, reposts, mentions, and press coverage. The most trusted form.

Paid Media

Ads, sponsored posts, and influencer partnerships.
