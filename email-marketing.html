Email Marketing

The highly effective digital marketing strategy of sending emails to prospects and customers. Effective email marketing converts prospects into customers, and turns one-time buyers into loyal, raving fans.

The Money is in the List

Deliverability

Getting into the inbox, not spam.

  • Authentication: SPF, DKIM, DMARC records are mandatory.
  • Sender Reputation: Don't buy lists. Keep bounce rates low.
  • Engagement: ISPs penalize you if no one opens your emails.
